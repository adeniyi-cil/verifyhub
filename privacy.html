Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 24 May 2026  |  Last Updated: 24 May 2026

VerifyHub ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Pi Network application that enables users to earn Pi cryptocurrency by completing human-verified micro-tasks. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use VerifyHub.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Pi Network Identity: Your Pi username and user ID provided through Pi SDK authentication upon login.
  • Task Activity Data: Records of tasks you view, attempt, and complete within the app.
  • Transaction Records: Pi payment amounts, transaction IDs, and timestamps associated with task completions.
  • Device Information: Basic device and browser data for app functionality and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Pi SDK and provide access to the app.
  • Assign, track, and verify task completions.
  • Process Pi payment rewards for completed tasks.
  • Maintain the integrity of our task verification system.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

3. Pi SDK and Authentication

VerifyHub uses the Pi Network SDK exclusively for user authentication. We do not collect your Pi wallet seed phrase, private keys, or any sensitive wallet credentials. All authentication is handled by the Pi Network platform. By using VerifyHub, you also agree to Pi Network's own Privacy Policy available at minepi.com/privacy-policy.

4. Pi Transactions

All payments within VerifyHub are made exclusively in Pi cryptocurrency. We do not process fiat currency or any other cryptocurrency. Transaction data is recorded for verification and dispute resolution purposes. VerifyHub does not store your Pi wallet balance or private financial information beyond what is needed to confirm task reward payments.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • With the Pi Network platform as required for SDK authentication and payment processing.
  • Where required by applicable law or regulatory authority.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of VerifyHub, its users, or the public.

6. Data Retention

We retain task completion records and transaction data for a period of 12 months to support dispute resolution and quality assurance. Account data associated with your Pi identity is retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your Pi Network account credentials.

8. Children's Privacy

VerifyHub is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice. Continued use of VerifyHub after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
